About Invertis:

Invertis University is a private university located in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, India.[2] It is situated on
Bareilly-Lucknow NH-24, 290 km from the national capital Delhi and 240 km from the state capital Lucknow. The Chancellor of the
university is Umesh Gautam (Mayor Bareilly & BJP politician) and the Vice-Chancellor is Y. D. S. Arya.[3]
